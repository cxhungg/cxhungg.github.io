I learnt C++ through making this simple physics engine! It can simulate projectile motion, collisions, springs, joints, and planets in orbit.

I loved working on this project and will continue to improve on it. Maybe adding more features like fluids, soft bodies, and electricity and magnetism!

Mathematical Model of Market Dynamics

Cryptocurrency markets have grown rapidly in size, complexity, and influence, and their interaction with traditional financial markets is becoming increasingly important.

In this project, I modeled the interactions between the two markets, first using a Markov Chain and then a modified Lotka-Volterra model, comparing the results of both approaches.

Network Anomaly Detection

Reducing dimensions and reconstructing them to find anomalies in network traffic? Yes please!

In this project, I used a combination of PCA and autoencoders to reduce the dimensionality of captured network traffic data and reconstruct it. Then I used the reconstructed data to detect anomalies in the network traffic.
